**Summary**  
In this conversation, Felix Braberg discusses the importance of bid floors in ad monetization. He highlights the shift from placements to bidding and the impact it has on ad revenue. Felix shares his findings on how bid floors can help optimize ad revenue for banners, interstitials, and rewarded video. He also explores the use of direct sold campaigns and alternative backfill options to maximize revenue. Overall, bid floors are becoming a crucial tool in ensuring higher value for impressions in the evolving ad monetization landscape.
