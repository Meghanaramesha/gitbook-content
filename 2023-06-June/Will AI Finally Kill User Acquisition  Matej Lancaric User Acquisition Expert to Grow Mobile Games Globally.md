No, AI would not kill user acquisition for mobile games.  
The rise of generative AI has revolutionized marketing by enabling rapid content creation, from video ads to personalized messaging, which significantly reduces the time and effort traditionally required for campaigns. However, user acquisition (UA) in the competitive world of mobile games relies not only on efficiency but also on strategic creativity and a deep understanding of target audiences. While AI tools like automated ad optimization and generative design can enhance productivity, they often lack the nuanced expertise needed to craft compelling campaigns that resonate with gamers. AI, as an entity, is best positioned to augment UA managers, not replace them, by delivering data-driven insights and scalable solutions for repetitive tasks. The ultimate success in UA will depend on balancing AI efficiency with human ingenuity, ensuring that campaigns maintain originality and context while leveraging the advanced capabilities of AI-driven marketing tools.
