##### **Summary**

This conversation delves into the significance of influencer marketing in mobile gaming, emphasizing the need for diversification in user acquisition (UA) channels. The speakers discuss the optimal timing for introducing influencer marketing, the types of games that benefit from it, and the importance of measuring return on investment (ROI). They also explore creative strategies for campaigns, the best platforms for influencer marketing, and the differences between traditional UA and influencer marketing. Budgeting strategies and post-campaign analysis are highlighted as essential components for successful influencer marketing efforts.

##### **Takeaways**

Influencer marketing is crucial for diversifying mobile game UA.  
Alternative UA channels are necessary due to changes in the market.  
The right time to introduce influencer marketing varies by game.  
Not all games benefit equally from influencer marketing.  
Measuring ROI is essential for evaluating campaign success.  
Creative strategies significantly impact the effectiveness of campaigns.  
YouTube and podcasts are effective platforms for influencer marketing.  
Traditional UA and influencer marketing have distinct differences.  
Budgeting for influencer marketing should be strategic and flexible.  
Post-campaign analysis provides valuable insights for future campaigns.
