## What is Capybara Go?

Capybara Go is a mobile roguelike adventure RPG developed by Habby. It focuses on exploration, strategy, and decision-making as players navigate the game as a capybara. The core gameplay involves user acquisition elements such as collecting items, customizing character builds, and engaging in random events. Players can unlock pets, acquire gear, and use skill damage tactics to progress through various stages, making each playthrough unique due to randomized events and outcomes.

Capybara Go allows players to experiment with different builds by using various items like the best rings, angel bow, and other gear to enhance performance. The game, Capybara Go involves choosing the right pets and mounts, which influence a player‚Äôs skill efficiency and versatility in battle. The game also includes a social element where players can change servers to connect with a broader audience, expanding the multiplayer experience.

In the context of user acquisition (UA) and monetization, Capybara Go encourages players to acquire resources like chest points, which can be used to unlock special items or pets. The game offers diversified elements for players to optimize their experience, making it an engaging option for the mobile gaming market.

Capybara GO is a unique game because it combines immersive gameplay mechanics, adorable capybara-themed characters, and engaging challenges that appeal to players of all ages.

Also, **Capybara GO is the latest update on the Habby template**, which eclectically takes all the proven mechanics from other big games and masterfully absorbs them into its signature metagame. Let‚Äôs follow this evolution piece by piece to understand it.

### **General Tips**

* Level all pets to 5,10,15.
* Use a tunic, guardian rings, and icy bubbles. And yes, your guardian rings and icy bubbles stack.
* **If you‚Äôre stuck, travel or spam tower.**
* Reset all gear levels and max out weapon > armor, then highest rarity S gear > highest rarity normal gear.
* Use elephant and ice fox.
* Use staff. Although you will hear of late-game players talking about how broken the bow is, the staff is undisputedly the best for newbies. This is because newbies will have low crit/combo/counter%, and weapons that give these stats cannot be utilized properly. For staff, everyone can utilize a flat skill dmg % increase as it will just give your rage skills/lightning/dagger, etc, more damage. Staff worked pretty well for me.
* The server resets at 8 am GMT+8 (Singapore time).
* Pull for mount and artifact in capymachine first before pulling for pets.
