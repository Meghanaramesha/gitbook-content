Key Takeaways for Creator Programs
----------------------------------

It‚Äôs no secret that these programs work. Managing a community is a huge undertaking, but focusing on creators has allowed massive programs like Supercell‚Äôs to be run by a team of 4. Here are some of the key takeaways to help inspire you to build one of your own:

Don‚Äôt focus on the follower count. Supercell has shown that investing in high-quality creators can pay off in the long run.
