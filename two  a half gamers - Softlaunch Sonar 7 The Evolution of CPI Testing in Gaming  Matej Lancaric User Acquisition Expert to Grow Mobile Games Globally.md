##### **Summary**

In this episode, the hosts delve into the evolving landscape of the gaming industry, focusing on CPI testing, game mechanics, and market trends. * CPI testing has evolved with mini games becoming the new standard.
* The gaming industry is experiencing significant disruptions, particularly in the Solitaire category.
* Innovations in game mechanics are crucial for standing out in a crowded market.

* Voodoo continues to innovate within the casual gaming space.
* Century Games is a company to watch due to their strategic approach.
* The Forex genre is becoming saturated with similar onboarding mechanics.
* Post-apocalyptic themes are gaining traction in mobile games.
