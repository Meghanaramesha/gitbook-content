##### **Summary**

The conversation explores the world of Chinese mini-games and their unique characteristics. The hosts discuss the rapid development and success of mini-games in China, highlighting the short development cycles, small team sizes, and high revenue potential. They delve into various genres and gameplay mechanics, such as tower defense rogue-like and 2D arcade idol combined with 4X. The hosts emphasize the importance of finding the right combination of genres and iterating on successful concepts. They also touch on the challenges of bringing Chinese mini-games to the Western market and the need for faster adoption and understanding of trends.

Chinese mini-games have short development cycles and high revenue potential
