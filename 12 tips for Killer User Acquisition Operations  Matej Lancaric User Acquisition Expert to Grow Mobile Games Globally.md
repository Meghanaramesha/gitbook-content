User Acquisition Operations refers to the strategic and systematic processes involved in attracting and acquiring new users for a product, service, or platform. This includes planning, executing, and optimizing marketing campaigns to reach target audiences effectively and convert them into active users or customers.

User acquisition (UA) for ad-based campaigns is a multi-faceted process that hinges on strategic diversification, data analysis, and creative alignment. Diversifying UA channels during soft launches and beyond is critical for balanced revenue streams and mitigating the risks of over-reliance on a single channel. Mastering cost-per-install (CPI) and AdROAS campaigns requires insights into mobile measurement partners (MMPs) like AppLovin and MAX, which enable advanced ROAS targeting and optimization.

Retention metrics, such as D1, D3, and D7, are essential for sustaining long-term success, while maximizing AdARPDAU through impression and DAU targeting can significantly enhance campaign performance. A seamless user experience relies on the alignment of creative production and app store optimization (ASO), emphasizing the collaboration between UA and art teams to balance data-driven performance with aesthetic appeal.

Experimentation, adaptation, and testing remain central to refining UA strategies. By leveraging diverse channels, optimizing campaigns for total ROAS, and focusing on retention and revenue metrics, businesses can ensure sustainable growth and profitability.
