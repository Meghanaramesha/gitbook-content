What makes Duolingo‚Äôs mobile user acquisition strategy so effective on social media?

### Understand trending formats and sounds

There are so many unspoken signals to a successful TikTok. The way they‚Äôre paced, the way they sound, the lighting, camera angles, and even where the captions are on the screen. ### Jab jab jab, right hook strategy
