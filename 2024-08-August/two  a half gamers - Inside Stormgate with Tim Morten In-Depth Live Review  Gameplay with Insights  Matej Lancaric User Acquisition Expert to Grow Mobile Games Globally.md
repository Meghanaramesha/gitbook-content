##### **Summary**

Frost Giant Studios is developing a new PC real-time strategy game called Storm Gate. The game follows the heritage of gameplay specific to Blizzard RTS, with resource collection, base building, and creating armies. The game has different modes, including campaign, cooperative, and competitive. Frost Giant Studios aims to avoid pay-to-win mechanics and grind, instead focusing on allowing players to try the game for free and then purchase additional content if they choose. The studio plans to release new campaign content regularly and is committed to listening to player feedback and improving the game. Stormgate is an early access game that is constantly evolving and improving. The game blends sensibilities from both Warcraft and Starcraft, appealing to a broad audience. The team behind Stormgate is committed to maintaining their independence and creating the best game possible. They have received support from investors and the community through crowdfunding campaigns. The future of Stormgate includes new co-op maps, more heroes, seasonal events, a map editor, and a learning tool system. The goal is to create a game that players enjoy and continue to purchase, similar to the longevity of Blizzard games.
