##### **Summary**

In this conversation, the hosts discuss various mobile games and highlight interesting ad placements and monetization strategies. They cover games in the hyper-casual and hybrid casual genres, showcasing examples of rewarded ad placements, banner ads, and app open interstitials. The hosts also discuss the effectiveness of these ad placements and their impact on user experience. The conversation explores the concept of app open rewarded interstitial ads and how they can be used to increase ad impressions and revenue. The hosts discuss the implementation of this ad format in the game Hexasaur and its potential impact on ad performance. They also touch on the use of AI-generated images from the Mid-Journey library for creative inspiration and the effectiveness of static images in Facebook advertising.

##### **Takeaways**

Different mobile games use various ad placements to monetize their apps  
Rewarded ad placements can be strategically placed to enhance the gameplay experience  
Banner ads can be conveniently located near core gameplay mechanics to increase click-through rates  
App open interstitials can generate revenue from users who show intent to play the game  
The effectiveness of ad placements depends on the number of sessions and session time App open rewarded interstitial ads can significantly increase ad impressions and revenue  
The game Hexasaur uses a clever implementation of app open rewarded interstitial ads to maximize ad exposure  
AI-generated images from the Mid-Journey library can provide creative inspiration for ad campaigns  
Static images are currently outperforming videos in Facebook advertising  
Regular creative refreshes, including the use of static images, are crucial for maintaining ad performance
