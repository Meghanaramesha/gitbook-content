How does SSSnaker‚Äôs ad monetization strategy compare to Survivor.io‚Äôs rewarded ad placements?
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SSSnaker‚Äôs ad monetization strategy as compared to Survivor.io‚Äôs rewarded ad placements¬†

SSSnaker has a very similar ad monetization strategy compared to Survivor.io which we discussed in detail on [#31](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mowIJKtG1M8&t=1985s&ab_channel=two%26ahalfgamers) of 2.5 Gamers below. In total, there are six rewarded ad placements inside of SSSnaker. Four ad placements are visible as part of the FTUE with another two placements that become visible after completing the first world.

How do rewarded ad placements in SSSnaker improve player engagement and retention?
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The rewarded ad placements in SSSnaker improve player engagement and retention are given below :

As part of the store SSSnaker has **4 Rewarded Ad placements**. One rewarded ad opens **a Normal Box** that‚Äôs on a five hour cool down timer. The second rewarded ad unit opens **a Super Box** which on a 4 day cool down timer. The third is a **20 gems for free** Rewarded ad placement on a 2 hour cool down. Last but not least there‚Äôs a **dynamic ad placement for gold** where users are encouraged to click for free gold to earn more gold by watching a Rewarded ad for even more free Gold.

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/SSSnaker-Ad-Monetization-Strategy-Google-Docs.png)  
![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/SSSnaker-Ad-Monetization-Strategy-Google-Docs-1.png)

That means as part of the **FTUE the max amount of Rewarded ads a user can watch is 4 RV impressions**. Once a player reaches the second world two more Rewarded Ad placements become visible for the user. One placement is a rewarded ad placement for revival which I suspect is the most popular ad placement in the game as you tend to die quite a lot in this game.

The second rewarded ad placement that becomes visible at the start of the second world is a **quick earnings booster** which allows you to earn rewards for watching a rewarded ad.

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/SSSnaker-Ad-Monetization-Strategy-Google-Docs-2.png)

**40% Ads revenue ($65k/day)**
------------------------------

In total that means that by the second world a user can watch 6+ Rewarded placements depending on how many times users choose to revive themselves in the main game play. If we combine the FTUE IMP/DAU along with the IMP/DAU after the first world in my experience we‚Äôre looking at an average **IMP/DAU of 3.8 with an ad viewer rate of 65%**. Taking the average eCPMs for rewarded videos for the same time period and combining it with SSSnakers active user base that gets an ad revenue of around **$65k/day.** Meaning that about 40% of SSSnakers total revenue comes from Rewarded Ads.

**Habby ‚Äì the hybrid-casual hit making machine**
------------------------------------------------

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Screen-Shot-2023-04-05-at-22.51.11-1024x344.png)

Last year (2022) September-October were **the** best months for the whole company. Making whooping **37 Million USD/month**. This is only IAP revenue and based on our assumptions (and calculations), they could be making **40-50% more on Ad revenue**. Survivor.io being the biggest hit so far. We also recorded a podcast about their recent global launch of SSSnaker:

In today‚Äôs mobile F2P gaming market, where (unfortunately) **99% of games** fail or don‚Äôt reach enough scale to be profitable, the key is to have **multiple shots on goal** at a fast frequency. Looking at their strategy, there are two key elements they use ‚Äì Unfolding gameplay and metagame framework. Let‚Äôs look at the first one now ‚Äì the **Unfolding gameplay**.

You can quickly see the principles behind this browser games, [paperclips](https://www.decisionproblem.com/paperclips/) where you will be first making innocent paperclips, and then a few hours later, you will be destroying the galaxy

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Habby-The-hit-making-machine-Final-Google-Docs.png)

**The Unfolding gameplay**
--------------------------

After they found their first success with ***Archero*** they have been perfecting this setup to this day, as we can see looking at their newest game. Not all games on their portfolio follow these patterns, so we are gonna explicitly look at ***Archero, Survivor.io, Punball, Kinja Run,*** and **SSSnaker.**

After the initial install, these games all look like **hyper-casual games**. They all strictly follow the rule of **one button control** in the form of a joystick, which controls the player‚Äôs character. You are immediately thrown into gameplay action, which is intuitive to control and get immediate enjoyment. After your initial run, you are taken to the main menu, and you upgrade your character through the **passive system,** the **only unlocked system**¬†at that time.

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Habby-The-hit-making-machine-Final-Google-Docs-1.png)

As seen in the screenshots, at this stage the game looks very simple, even though we can expect some more systems under the locked icons. Player then continues the main loop and the situation repeats. At some point the game unlocks **equipment inventory** and slowly drops more and more features on the player. But we are talking multiple days of sessions, not your usual **20 minutes tutorial** which unlocks the whole UI on you afterwards.

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Habby-The-hit-making-machine-Final-Google-Docs-2.png)

Below we can see how the UI is slowly filling with more and more features and systems. You can see how my ***SSSnaker*** has progressed after a few days, but I still **haven‚Äôt unlocked all of the main features** that the game has to offer. On the other hand, here is my ***Survivor.io*** account which is pretty much fully **unfolded** compared to its initial stage we have seen here before. Another example is seen with the Punball progressed account.

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Habby-The-hit-making-machine-Final-Google-Docs-3.png)

This process makes sure that the player‚Äôs attention **slowly shifts** from mastering the core gameplay into **RPG progression** of his character. In the end you are upgrading **multiple systems** and playing **different game modes** to get more power into your character and move onto the next stage. On the UI below we can clearly see that this is not anymore the hypercasual game we have started with. It morphed into a very heavy IAP driven **Roguelite ARPG**.

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Habby-The-hit-making-machine-Final-Google-Docs-4.png)  
![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Habby-The-hit-making-machine-Final-Google-Docs-5.png)

This enables Habby to widen the **UA funnel** getting hypercasual-like reach and CPIs. This principle is an upgrade to the usual **hypercasual pipeline** where small studios send prototypes to the publisher, which then tests the KPIs and picks the ones with the best metrics, and then pushes them with UA. Habby not only does this, but they actively **change the feature pacing** of these prototypes to fit their unfolding gameplay formula. They slot the core gameplay into their **metagame framework,** which is a tried and tested progression of a rogue-lite Action RPG. Let‚Äôs look into detail how this transformation works. ***SSSnaker*** was published by Habby but developed by Yiduo Games under the former name of ***Snake Master.***

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Habby-The-hit-making-machine-Final-Google-Docs-6.png)

You can play the game through an old [build APK](https://apksos.com/app/com.lark.snake.fire) on Android if you want to see the differences for yourself!

The first big difference is the **equipment progression.** In the original game there is just a simple upgrade sink compared to the 6 slot equipment gacha which is one of the main pillars of the metagame framework. It is presented in every one of these games.

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Habby-The-hit-making-machine-Final-Google-Docs-7.png)

***Survivor.io*** even went so far that they doubled the inventory right after global launch, probably to immediately get more **spend depth** into the game as they saw the amazing global launch numbers.Same gacha system progression applies also here.

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Habby-The-hit-making-machine-Final-Google-Docs-8.png)

In the first session of ***Snake Master,*** you could already select a different snake with different rarities to collect. In ***SSSnaker*** this feature is unlocked somewhere around day 4 if you play a lot. This was changed to fit the **unfolding formula** as RPG elements are pushed for later in order for players to focus on **mastering core gameplay**.

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Habby-The-hit-making-machine-Final-Google-Docs-9.png)

Another staple of the framework is the **idle generation mechanic**. Not only does it incentivize sessions, but it further **reinforces the roguelite nature** of the gameplay. Progression of roguelite games is based on the principle that even though you always start your new run from **level 1 character** you get resources into permanent upgrades that carry over between the runs. As the game is based around Player versus environment difficulty, this assures that **eventually you progress** even in chapters you are stuck in or can‚Äôt deal with the mechanics. Your stats grow so high that the content that was hard to get through will eventually **become easy** with your permanent upgrades. This makes for a very approachable and ‚Äúcasual‚Äù progression.

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Habby-The-hit-making-machine-Final-Google-Docs-10.png)

As seen on the big UI layout of Survivor.io image we have shown before the monetization and live-ops systems are the last to unlock in their full force with all the battle passes, achievements, subscriptions, daily quests, daily rewards, piggy banks, offer systems and event systems.These are again universal for the whole **framework**.

All of these progression layers such as talents, equipment, additional hero characters create very deep **IAP** and **Ad** economy **spend depth** that is similar to a **midcore RPG**.

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Habby-The-hit-making-machine-Final-Google-Docs-11.png)

This whole process enables Habby to focus on their **metagame** **framework**, improving even the older games as it is compatible. In the meantime they continue testing¬† prototypes of **different core gameplays** from different studios to get the best KPIs to slot into their framework and keep up with their very fast release frequency fueling the ‚Äúhit machine‚Äù and getting multiple shots on goal. You could say that Habby are the true masters of **‚ÄúHybridCasual‚Äù**.
