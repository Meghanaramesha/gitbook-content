What is MY perfect Hotel?
-------------------------

My perfect hotel is a actually a pretty good & fun game!

**My Perfect Hotel** is a (hybrid) casual game where you‚Äôre in charge of managing a hotel complex where you‚Äôll accommodate more and more guests. Keeping in mind all the tasks that appear throughout the game, you must try to please all the visitors so that they leave satisfied with the facilities.

What User Acquisition Strategy Drove ‚ÄòMy Perfect Hotel‚Äô to 13 Million Installs?
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The user acquisition strategy that drove My Perfect Hotel achieved 13 million installs in one month by leveraging a targeted user acquisition (UA) strategy, focusing heavily on high-impact markets like the US and other Tier 1 countries. Interesting is the fact that this game was launched last year on August 22 (no soft-launch on iOS), only on Android.

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/Screen-Shot-2023-09-20-at-22.52.43.png)

Let‚Äôs talk about the [soft launch](https://lancaric.me/how-to-soft-launch-a-mobile-game-in-2022/) a little bit. They soft-launched the game on Android in April 2022 and stayed in SL until August when they launched it globally. They used the classic hypercasual approach, **US only**. Looks like they only published five new versions and the last one in July was the release candidate, which went global.

In August the global launch month, they achieved a nice 3.5 mil installs on GP and 1.6 mil installs on iOS.

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/41VeksyxGL._SR600315_PIWhiteStripBottomLeft035_SCLZZZZZZZ_FMpng_BG255255255.png)

Regarding revenues, $100k in In App Purchases (= don‚Äôt forget our [sadness multiplier](https://www.tiktok.com/@two.and.a.half.gamers/video/7277281708783783200?is_from_webapp=1&sender_device=pc&web_id=7114254672734504450) => at least 200k) which increased to $286k resp, around $500k IAP per month with six mil installs IOS + GP (70% GP obviously)

[![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/hybrid-casual-1.png)](https://open.substack.com/pub/lancaric/p/hybrid-casual-ua-playbook-real-data?r=7qqaf&utm_campaign=post&utm_medium=web&showWelcome=true)

Then, they scaled down in Feb this year until August 2023. At that time SayGames UA team was scaling Dreamdale in April/May, so my assumption would be that they took their UA learnings from this game and applied them to My Perfect Hotel.

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/Screen-Shot-2023-09-20-at-22.58.05.png)  
BOOM! My perfect hotel achieved a staggering **13 MILLION installs** in 1 month and already has 10 million when writing about it. Mostly buying traffic in the US, but also some other Tier1 countries:

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/Screen-Shot-2023-09-20-at-22.52.30.png)

How Did ‚ÄòMy Perfect Hotel‚Äô Use a Diverse Channel Mix to Scale User Acquisition?

My Perfect Hotel‚Äô used a diverse channel mix in following ways :

**Applovin**is the biggest channel in terms of spending on both iOS and Android.

* Not surprising at all. We talked about this multiple times in the past. Applovin is killing it at the moment on multiple fronts: UA and Admon!
* We can see both videos and playables in their creative mix. But 50% of their overall spend goes to playable on Applovin!
* It‚Äôs not a coincidence Applovin is the biggest channel for SayGames.

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/Screenshot-2023-09-18-at-12.27.21-PM.png)

This is an old case study, but I believe it started a very fruitful cooperation and relationship between companies. Quoting from the [case study:](https://www.applovin.com/success-stories/saygames/)

*SayGames can now scale user acquisition profitably by moving away from campaigns where bids are optimized based on the performance of each source app to AppDiscovery‚Äôs Ad ROAS campaigns. This intelligent user-based bidding system allows advertisers to set their ROAS goal and bid accurately based on the value of each user, regardless of source, to maximize both profit and scale.*

***Additionally, they also implemented MAX in-app bidding to build a fully automated monetization stack that optimizes for higher returns. MAX and AppDiscovery sync seamlessly, making it possible for SayGames to automate all buying and to optimize for ROAS‚Äî with their single largest network partner.***

This is very very important. As soon as they implemented MAX, they could scale rapidly all their hypercasual titles thanks to the AdROAS campaigns. But as you know, the hottest UA shit right now is ‚Ä¶. the BLENDED ROAS! Which combines Ad revenue and In-app purchase revenue! This is possible only if you have MAX mediation. What types of games now have 50:50 split Ads vs IAPs? Exactly, hybrid casual ‚Äì My Perfect Hotel!

What Role Does LTV and CPI Play in Scaling ‚ÄòMy Perfect Hotel‚Äô as a Hybrid Casual Game?

![](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/prezka-obrazok_2-1-1024x630.png)

The role of LTV and CPI in scaling My perfect Hotel is as followed :

Yes. Again! Scaling a game is not only a function of a [killer user acquisition](https://lancaric.me/11-tips-for-killer-user-acquisition-ops-q1-2023/) operation. It is also a function of an LTV. You can only scale your budget until the LTV allows you. Eg. If your LTV is $5, you can run profitable campaigns until you hit $4.5 CPI (or any other CPI that you calculate based on your margins). Why does My Perfect Hotel do so well? I worked on some Idle and hypercasual games before.

Generally, they have lower CPIs than other categories. No shit! Finally, **hypercasual games** **drive low CPIs**. I was in a lot of discussion about how ‚Äúfancy or more quality‚Äù visual style drives higher IAPs because of the premium feel of the game.¬†The visual style doesn‚Äôt have any impact on IAP.

**Monetization**

Looking at the previous builds of My Perfect Hotel, you can clearly see some major iterations on the metagame, such as adding specific currencies per each hotel, together with more hotels added. This was not the case before, as this can be seen as a clear push towards hybrid elements.

* **Soft currency tied only to one hotel ‚Äì non-transferable between different hotels**
  + **This was not the case before (major update!)**
  + Prestige currency from leveling that can be used across other hotels but not in the current version!

Recently we have seen Skip-it‚Äôs, the new currency sold directly for money for skipping rewarded videos. My Perfect Hotel directly uses his hard currency instead of Skip-it‚Äôs, which then greatly increases the possible spend depth and is also much easier to understand.

There is already another game called King of Fail on SayGames‚Äôs account, which clearly continues this trend of the Arcade Idle genre. It is more and more clear that they are successfully building a template for this genre as you can see on the image, which currently puts them in second place, right after [Habby in the Hybridcasual race](https://lancaric.me/hybrid-casual-sssnaker-habby-post-idfa-ua/).

How Did ‚ÄòMy Perfect Hotel‚Äô Use Competitor Insights to Develop Successful Playable Ads?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[<https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/Creative-Gallery-‚Äî-Ad-Intelligence-‚Äî-Sensor-Tower-4.mp4>](https://lancaric.me/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/Creative-Gallery-‚Äî-Ad-Intelligence-‚Äî-Sensor-Tower-4.mp4?_=15)

My Perfect Hotel‚Äô used a competitor-focused insights to develop successful playable ads in following ways

1. Find a very strong competitor and look into the audience overlap.
2. Put a target on their back and assess all their creatives with the most impressions.
3. Look for recent winners (last 30 days) and evergreen concepts in the last year.
4. Don‚Äôt reinvent the wheel.
5. Produce 3-5 creatives (could be 1:1 exact copy)
6. Iterate winners from there!
